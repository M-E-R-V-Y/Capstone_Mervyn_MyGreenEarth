What is a wastage?



All that goes into the trash !




Yes ! Your leftover meals, the bottle, the can, the oily pizza box, the old notebook, the candy wrap, the broken toy, the worn pair of sneakers ... whatever you throw in the trash at home, you obviously know that this is waste!

Your broken bike, the old mattress, the broken table, the broken computer ... which do not necessarily fit in the trash and which you take to the recycling center, are also waste. So that's all we throw away in our daily and family life!

Companies also produce it and in large quantities!
